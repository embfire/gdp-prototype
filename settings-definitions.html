Lifecycle stages classify identified guests by visit behavior. Each guest belongs to exactly one stage at any given time. Rename stages and adjust the thresholds below to match your brand's definition of guest activity.

First-time

info
Automatically assigned on a guest's first visit ever. No configurable thresholds.

Returning

days

Guests with 2 visits whose last visit falls within this window. Beyond it, they move to Churned.

Loyal

visits

Guests who reach this visit count and remain active within their personal visit cadence are considered Loyal.

At-Risk

× average inter-visit gap

A guest is At-Risk when they've been absent for longer than this multiple of their own average gap between visits.

days since last visit

Applied to guests below the Loyal minimum visit count, who don't yet have a personal visit cadence to calculate against.

Win-back

info
Automatically assigned on a guest's first visit after an At-Risk or Churned period. No configurable thresholds.

Churned

days since last visit

Guests inactive beyond this window are considered Churned. Typically matches the Returning "last visit within" window.
